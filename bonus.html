Groot

List of Groot Super Powers

  1. Groot can absorb wood as food, and has the ability to regenerate
  2. Groot can control trees and plants, using them to attack others, and appears to be resistant to fire
  3. Groot is able to sprout dramatically increasing mass which then severely inhibits movement.
  4. Groot has been seemingly killed on multiple occasions, each time regrowing from a sprig.
