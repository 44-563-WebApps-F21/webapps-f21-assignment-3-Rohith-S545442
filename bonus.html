The Red Godzilla

Godzilla can emit atomic energy in all directions from every inch of his body in a short-range pulse called the nuclear pulse. Godzilla used this ability throughout the Heisei Series

Godzilla acquired the red spiral heat ray after Rodan made a similar sacrificial move. Rodan's power gave him this new attack, which he was able to use to take down Mechagodzilla. The power-up also didn't go away once the fight was over

Directions to the lair of Godzilla

  1. The Godzilla's lair is a cave within the Hollow Earth.
  2. It is the headquarters of Godzilla and, as such, provides a place of shelter, healing, and history.
